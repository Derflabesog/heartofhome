Our Core Services

Plot & Plan Construction

We offer complete plot & plan construction solutions that allow clients to build a home on an approved plot with a fixed, structured process.

What This Includes:
  • Site evaluation and planning
  • House plan selection or customization
  • Cost estimation and budgeting
  • Construction management
  • Final handover

Ideal For: First-time homebuyers and families seeking a guided construction process.

House Design & Architecture

We provide functional, modern house designs that balance affordability, comfort, and long-term usability.

What This Includes:
  • Standard house designs
  • Custom house plans
  • Space planning and layout optimization
  • Design adjustments to suit budgets

Note: Design services can be offered as standalone or combined with construction.

Civil Engineering Services

Our engineering expertise ensures your home has a solid foundation, proper drainage, and meets all municipal standards.

What This Includes:
  • Foundation design and construction
  • Structural works
  • Concrete and reinforcement works
  • Technical site assessments

Key Advantage: Engineer-led execution ensures compliance, safety, and long-term durability.

Foundation & Structural Construction

We specialize in strong, durable foundation systems suitable for local soil conditions, including rock-face foundations.

What This Includes:
  • Rock-face foundations
  • Reinforced concrete foundations
  • Ground preparation
  • Load-bearing structural systems

Site Supervision & Project Management

We provide professional supervision throughout the construction process to ensure quality workmanship and adherence to approved plans.

What This Includes:

  • Contractor coordination
  • Quality control inspections

  • Progress reporting

Renovations & Extensions
