Preventative maintenance is cheaper than emergency repairs. Here is a checklist for Namibian homeowners to keep their property in top shape.

Before the Rainy Season

  • Clean Gutters: Remove leaves and sand to prevent blockages and overflow.
  • Check Roof Seals: Inspect flashings and ridge capping for cracks. Re-seal with waterproofing membrane if needed.
  • Clear Drains: Ensure yard drainage is clear to prevent water pooling near foundations.

Dry Season Checks

  • Paint Inspection: The Namibian sun is harsh. Check for peeling paint on wood and metal surfaces to prevent rot and rust.
  • Pest Control: Look for signs of termites, especially in wooden door frames and roof trusses.
