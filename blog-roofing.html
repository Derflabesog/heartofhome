Roofing Materials for Hot Climates

Posted by Heart of Home ‚Ä¢ Construction & DIY

In Namibia, your roof does more than keep the rain out‚Äîit's your primary defense against the intense sun. Choosing the right material can significantly impact your home's internal temperature.

IBR Sheeting (Metal)

Inverted Box Rib (IBR) sheeting is durable, lightweight, and cost-effective. However, metal conducts heat. If you choose IBR, proper insulation (like Alububble or IsoBoard) is non-negotiable to keep your home cool.

Concrete Tiles

Tiles have high thermal mass, meaning they absorb heat slowly during the day and release it at night. They are excellent for temperature regulation but require a stronger roof truss structure due to their weight.

Color Matters

Lighter roof colors reflect more sunlight. A white or light grey roof can reduce attic temperatures by up to 10¬∞C compared to a dark charcoal roof.
