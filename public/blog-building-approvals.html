Understanding Building Approvals in Namibia (Simple Guide)

Category: Construction 101

Before any construction begins, you must navigate the approval process. It ensures your home is safe, legal, and compliant with local regulations.

Required Approvals

  • Architectural Drawings: Detailed plans showing the layout, elevations, and site plan must be submitted to the municipality.
  • Structural Engineer Sign-off: An engineer must certify the foundation and structural elements.
  • Municipal Compliance: The local authority checks that your plans meet zoning and building codes.

Note: Building without approval is illegal and can result in fines or demolition orders.
