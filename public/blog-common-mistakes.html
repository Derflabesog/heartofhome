Common Building Mistakes First-Time Homeowners Make

Category: Construction 101

Building a home is complex. Avoid these common pitfalls to ensure a smooth project.

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Choosing the Cheapest Contractor: Low quotes often mean low quality or hidden costs later. Look for value and reputation, not just the lowest price.
  • Ignoring Soil Tests: Building on the wrong foundation for your soil type is a recipe for disaster.
  • Poor Communication: Assume nothing. Ensure everything is written down in the contract.
  • No Written Contracts: Never start work without a signed agreement detailing scope, costs, and timelines.
