Affordable Home Designs That Work Well in Namibia’s Climate

Category: Design Ideas

Namibia has a harsh climate with hot summers and cold winters. Your home design should work with the environment, not against it, to save on energy costs.

Design Features for Namibia

  • Simple Roof Designs: Complex roofs are expensive and prone to leaks. Simple gable or mono-pitch roofs are cost-effective and durable.
  • Orientation: Orient your home north-facing to maximize natural light and warmth in winter while keeping it cool in summer.
  • Shaded Windows: Use roof overhangs or awnings to protect windows from direct midday sun.
  • Light Colors: Light-colored exterior walls reflect heat, keeping the interior cooler.
