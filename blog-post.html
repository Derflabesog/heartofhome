5 Things to Know Before Building Your First Home

Posted on 12 Jan 2025 • DIY & Construction

Building your first home is exciting but requires proper planning. This guide helps Namibian families avoid common mistakes.

1. Know Your Budget

Always include contingency costs. Material prices can change during construction.

2. Understand Your Soil & Foundation

Rock-face foundations are common in Namibia and provide durability.

3. Plan for Future Extensions

Even small homes should allow space for growth.

4. Safety First

DIY work should never compromise safety. Consult professionals where needed.

5. Get Professional Guidance

Engineers and experienced builders save you money long-term.
