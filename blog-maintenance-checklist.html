Essential Home Maintenance Checklist for New Homeowners

Category: Maintenance & Repairs

Your home is an investment. Regular maintenance prevents small issues from becoming expensive repairs.

Annual Checklist

  • Roof Inspection: Check for loose tiles or sheeting, especially before the rainy season.
  • Clean Gutters: Blocked gutters can cause water overflow and damage your walls or foundation.
  • Plumbing Check: Look for leaks under sinks and around toilets. Fix dripping taps immediately.
