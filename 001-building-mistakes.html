Building your first home is an exciting milestone, but it requires careful planning to avoid costly surprises. This guide helps Namibian families navigate the process and avoid common mistakes.

1. Not Understanding Your Budget

Your total cost isn't just the building quote. Always factor in a contingency fund (10-15%) for unexpected costs. Remember to budget for municipal fees, utility connections (water/electricity), and landscaping.

2. Ignoring Soil & Foundation

This is the most critical part of your house. A cheap foundation is a recipe for future disaster. In many parts of Namibia, a proper soil assessment is necessary to determine the right foundation, preventing cracks and structural failure down the line.
